\veheading{Christe qui lux est et dies.}
\gregorioscore{commons/lent/compline/hymn.gabc}

\begin{multicols}{2}
	\interlinepenalty=10000
	
	2. All holy Lord, we pray to thee,\\
	Keep us tonight from danger free;\\
	Grant us, dear Lord, in thee to rest,\\
	so be our sleep in quiet blest.
	
	3. Let us dull sleep the soul oppress,\\
	Nor crafty foe the heart possess:\\
	Nor Satan's wiles the flesh allure,\\
	To make us in thy sight impure.
	
	4. And while our eyes soft slumber take,\\
	Still be the heart to thee awake;\\
	By thy right hand upheld above\\
	Thy servants resting in thy love.
	
	5. Yea, our Defender, by thou night\\
	To bid the pow'rs of darkness fly;\\
	Keep us from sin, and guide for good\\
	Thy servants purchased by thy Blood.
	
	6. Remember us, dear Lord, we pray,\\
	While in this mortal flesh we stay:\\
	'Tis thou who dost the soul defend\\
	Be present with us to the end.
	
	7. All laud to God the Father be;\\
	All praise, eternal Son, to thee:\\
	All glory, as is ever meet,\\
	To God the Holy Paraclete. Amen.
\end{multicols}
