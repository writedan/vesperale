\veheading{\textit{Immense caeli Conditor}.}
\gregorioscore{monday/immense_caeli_conditor.gabc}

\begin{multicols}{2}
	\interlinepenalty=10000
	
	2. The floods above thou didst ordain;\\
	The floods below thou didst restrain:\\
	That moisture might attempter heat,\\
	Lest the parched earth should ruin meet.
	
	3. Upon our souls, good Lord, bestow\\
	The gift of grace in endless flow:\\
	Lest some renewed deceit or wile\\
	Of former sin should us beguile.
	
	4. Let faith discover heav'nly light;\\
	So shall its rays direct us right:\\
	And let this faith each error chase,\\
	And never give to falsehood place.
	
	\instruct{Until the Purification}\\
	5. All honor, laud, and glory be,\\
	O Jesu, Virgin-born, to thee:\\
	All glory, as is ever meet,\\
	To Father and to Paraclete. Amen.
	
	\instruct{After the Purification}\\
	5. O Father, that we ask be done,\\
	Through Jesus Christ, thine only Son:\\
	Who, with the Holy Ghost and thee,\\
	Doth live and reign eternally. Amen.
\end{multicols}
